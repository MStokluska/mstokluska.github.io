Choosing the career just for you

Did you know that in some countries a person requalifies on average 3 times in life time? Did you ever thought about going back to college and change your proffesion? We, at Life Matter, have some interesting information to help you get back to college in Ireland! As we do understand that the entire process might be overwhelming, we have decided to put all the relevant information on one site. We also offer one-to-one meetings where a guidance plan will be created to meet your needs and help you to get back to education in Ireland. Please have a look at the testimonials we have received from people whom we have helped so far here. Many people at some point in their lives have no idea what to do, what would the like to do but they know that it's not what they currently doing. At Life Matter our focus is You, Your personal development is our priority thus we offer guidance to all the mature people who whish to get back to third level education. We do realize that many of you have most likely families, commitments and many expenses, however, we would like to take this opportunity and show you that the entire process of going back to education, with a bit of financial support, can be very easy. Just by being on our site you already made steps toward your dreams, all we hope for is to help you reach your destination by providing guidance and accurate information. First off, if you are unsure what are your emplyement predispositions we suggest to contact us, to make it easier for you just fill in form on the last page and we will contact you! Alternatively visit some of the websites we have picked for you, if you however know very well what course are you interested in, click next button on the bottom of the page to preceed to next step or simply, click on Social Welfare.
